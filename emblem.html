Emblem Guide

Emblems are hero enhancements that grant additional buffs or effects as well as bonus attributes. In the preparation tab or during the hero pick phase, players can choose their preferred emblem sets. Currently, there are 7 types of emblems, each with its own set of bonuses.

Standard talents and core talents are the two of talents. Players can choose additional effects for their chosen hero via talents, providing them with an advantage with the suitable configurations; however, they can only choose two standard talents and one core talent. Currently, there are 16 types of standard talents and 8 types of core talents, each with its own set of bonuses.


Basic Common Emblem Set

Basic Common Emblem

◇ Attributes
● Hybrid Regen:

◇ Standard Talents
Thrill
Gain 16 Adaptive Attack.
Swift
Gain 10% Extra Attack Speed.
Wilderness Blessing
Movement Speed in the Jungle and River is increased by 10%. This bonus is halved when in combat with enemy heroes, Lord, or Turtle.
Seasoned Hunter
Damage against Lord and Turtle is increased by 15%. This bonus is halved against regular Creeps and doesn't affect the damage of Retribution.

◇ Core Talents
Impure Rage
Dealing damage with skills deals 4% of target's Max HP extra Adaptive Damage and restores 2% Mana on hit. (If the hero does not use Mana, restores 1% HP instead, and deals up to 120 Damage to non-hero units. Cooldown: 5 seconds.)
Quantum Charge
Dealing damage with Basic Attacks increases Movement Speed by 30% for 1.5s and restores 75-180 HP (scales with level). Cooldown: 8 seconds.
War Cry
After every 3 consecutive damage dealt to enemy heroes with separate Basic Attacks or skills, all damage dealt increases by 8% for 6 seconds. (A single Basic Attack or skill that deals damage multiple times only counts as 1. Cooldown: 6 seconds.)
Temporal Reign
.
The next Ultimate cast decreases other active skills' remaining cooldowns by 1.5 times within 4 seconds. (This only applies to the hero themselves. Cooldown: 20 seconds.)
