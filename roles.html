Mobile Legends: Bang Bang Roles

A role or class is a category that your character will play throughout the game. A total of 125 heroes is available at this moment, which come from 6 classes â€“ Tank, Fighter, Assassin, Mage, Marksman , and Support. Each of them has their unique stats and ability, but some of them are stronger than others.

Every hero in Mobile Legends is unique, has a different set of abilities, and overall has different ways to help win the game. But first, let's talk about roles first. Roles are assigned within the Heroes roles that your character will play throughout the game. There 6 Roles/Classes.

Fighter

Fighters are versatile heroes that excel in both offense and defense, often taking on the role of secondary damage dealers or tanks.

Assassin

Assassins are agile and stealthy, specializing in quickly eliminating key enemy targets, usually the damage dealers or supports.

Marksman

Marksmen are the primary ranged damage dealers in the game, capable of dealing high damage from a distance but often fragile.

Tank

Tanks are robust heroes with high durability, designed to absorb damage and protect their teammates in fights.

Support

Supports provide healing, buffs, or crowd control to assist their team, ensuring their teammates survive and succeed in battles.

Mage

Provide magical damage and utility to the team such as crowd control , healing and buffs.
