Roaming Items

Conceal

Provides Movement Speed and has an active skill to hide yourself from the mini map.

Encourage

Provides Movement Speed and Damage Reduction for the team after use.

Favor

Provides Movement Speed and Cooldown Reduction after use.

Dire Hit

Provides Movement Speed and activates critical attacks.

Jungle Items

Flame Retribution

Provides HP regeneration and has an active skill to deal Flame Magic Damage to forest monsters.

Ice Retribution

Provides Mana regeneration and has an active skill to provide Freeze Magic Damage to forest monsters.

Bloody Retribution

Provides HP regeneration and deals damage to forest monsters.

Movement/Boots Items

Warrior Boots

Increases Movement Speed and provides Physical Defense.

Demon Shoes

Increases Movement Speed and provides Mana regeneration.

Rapid Boots

Increases Movement Speed and provides additional Movement Speed when not taking damage.

Swift Boots

Increases Movement Speed and provides additional Attack Speed.

Magic Shoes

Increases Movement Speed and provides Cooldown Reduction.

Arcane Boots

Increases Movement Speed and provides Magical PEN.

Tough Boots

Increases Movement Speed and provides Crowd Control Reduction.

Attack Items

Sea Halberd

Provides Physical Attack and Attack Speed, with a unique passive that reduces the opponent's Shield and HP Regen after dealing damage.

Rose Gold Meteor

Provides Physical Attack, Magical Defense, and Physical Lifesteal. Its passive creates a Shield when HP falls below 30%.

War Axe

provides an adequate amount of HP, cooldown reduction, an improving physical attack, and a much appreciated spell vamp and true damage.

Hunter Strike

Provides Physical Attack, Cooldown Reduction, and Physical PEN. Its passive increases Movement Speed after dealing damage to an opponent.

Blade of Despair

Provides Physical Attack and Movement Speed. Its passive increases Physical Attack when attacking an opponent with HP below 50%.

Blade of the Heptaseas

Provides Physical Attack, HP, and Physical PEN. Its passive provides additional Physical Damage to Basic Attacks if it does not receive or deal damage within 5 seconds.

Great Dragon Spear

This piece of equipment is suitable for heroes relying on Crit Chance and CD Reduction to improve their mobility.

Windtalker

Provides Attack Speed, Movement Speed, and Critical Chance. Its passive deals Magic Damage to Basic Attacks to several opponents.

Endless Battle

Provides Physical Attack, Mana Regen, HP, Cooldown Reduction, Movement Speed, and Physical Lifesteal. Its passive deals True Damage after using skills.

Berserker's Fury

Provides Physical Attack, Critical Chance, and Critical Damage. Its passive increases Physical Attack after Critical Hit.

Haas's Claws

Provides Physical Attack and Physical Lifesteal. Its passive provides additional Physical Lifesteal when HP is below 50%.

Malefic Roar

Provides Physical Attack and Physical PEN. Its passive increases Physical Penetration when dealing damage to opponents.

Malefic Gun

Best equipped by Marksmen to increase their Attack Range for kiting. provides some attack speed and movement speed to boost Marksmen's damage. It is also effective against opponents with a high level of physical defense.

Magic Items

Genius Wand

Gives Magic Power, Movement Speed, and Magical PEN. Its passive reduces the opponent's Magic Defense when dealing damage.

Lightning Truncheon

Provides Magic Power, Mana, and Cooldown Reduction. Its passive provides Magic Damage to the next skill.

Fleeting Time

Provides Magic Power, Mana, and Cooldown Reduction. Its passive reduces Ultimate Skill Cooldown after eliminating or getting an Assist.

Blood Wings

Provides Magic Power and HP. Its passive provides Shield after gaining HP.

Clock of Destiny

Gives Magic Power, HP, and Mana. Its passive increases HP and Magic Attack every few seconds.

Starlium Scythe.

ideal for magic heroes who perform a basic Attack basic Attack immediately or who have skills that trigger attack effects on-hit after casting a skill. Its including magic power, cooldown reduction, hybrid lifesteal, movement speed and mana regen

Glowing Wand

Provides Magic Power, HP, and Movement Speed. Its passive provides a Burn effect on targets when using skills.

Ice Queen Wand

Gives Magic Power, Magical Lifesteal, Mana, and Movement Speed. Its passive provides a slow effect when receiving damage from an opponent's skill.

Concentrated Energy

Provides Magic Power, HP, and Magical Lifesteal. Its passive restores HP after eliminating an opponent.

Holy Crystal

Gives Magic Power. Its passive increases Magic Attack.

Divine Glaive

Gives Magic Power and Magical PEN. Its passive increases Magic Penetration when dealing damage to opponents.

Wishing Lantern

"Butterfly Goddess" works like a projectile similar to a ranged basic attack projectile. It'll chase the target enemy and won't deal damage until it reaches the enemy.

Feather of Heaven

Best equipped by Magic DPS heroes whose Basic Attacks have special effects.

Winter Crown

A really good item against high burst damage heroes since you will become untargetable and invincible for 2 seconds. It also provides some adaptive attack and cooldown reduction to avoid losing too much damage.

Enchanted Talisman

Provides Magic Power, HP, and Cooldown Reduction. Its passive provides Mana regeneration.

Flash of the Oasis

Provides Magic Power, HP, and Cooldown Reduction. Its unique passive gets a shield.

Defense Items

Radiant Armor

Provides HP, Magical Defense, and HP Regen. Its passive increases Magic Damage Reduction when receiving Magic Damage.

Twilight Armor

Provides HP, Mana, and Critical Damage Reduction. Its passive reduces physical damage received and deals true damage to nearby enemies.

Brute Force Breastplate

Provides HP, Physical Defense, and Movement Speed. Its passive increases Movement Speed and Physical & Magic Defense after using a skill or Basic Attack.

Immortality

Provides HP and Physical Defense. Its passive allows heroes to come back to life after being eliminated.

Dominance Ice

Provides Mana, Physical Defense, Movement Speed, and Cooldown Reduction. Its passive reduces Attack Speed and HP Regen of nearby opponents.

Athena Shield

Provides HP, Magical Defense, and HP Regen. Its passive provides a Shield when receiving Magic Damage.

Oracle

Provides HP, Magical Defense, and Cooldown Reduction. Its passive increases Shield Absorption and HP Regen.

Antique Cuirass

Provides HP, Physical Defense, and HP Regen. Its passive reduces opponent's Physical and Magical Attack.

Guardian Helmet

Provides HP and HP Regen. Its passive provides extra HP Regen when HP is below 40%.

Cursed Helmet

Deals HP, Magic Defense, and Magic Damage to the surrounding area. Its passive deals Magic Damage to nearby opponents.

Thunder Belt

Provides HP, Mana, Physical Defense, and Cooldown Reduction. Its passive provides True Damage and Slow effect when using skills.

Queen's Wings

Gives HP and Physical Lifesteal. Its passive provides Damage Reduction when HP is below 40%.

Blade Armor

Provides Physical Defense and has a unique passive that deals damage back to the opponent when attacking.
